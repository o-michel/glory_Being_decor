our services

Designing event decor

We work with clients to design and create a theme for an event. We interact with our client roster in the selection of colors, lighting, centerpieces, linens, furniture, and other decorative elements to create a cohesive and visually appealing look..

Sourcing and renting decor

We may also source and rent decor items for their clients, such as table linens, chairs, drapes, lighting fixtures, and centerpieces.

Setting up and taking down decor

On the day of the event, we are responsible for setting up all the decor items and ensuring that everything is in its place. After the event, they will dismantle and remove all the decor items.

Managing vendors

We may also work with other vendors, such as florists, caterers, and rental companies, to ensure that all aspects of the decor are coordinated and executed properly.

Budget management

As an event decorator, we may also help the client stay within their budget by suggesting cost-effective decor solutions and negotiating with vendors to get the best prices.

Providing advice and guidance

Throughout the planning process, an event decorator may provide advice and guidance to the client on decor choices, logistics, and other aspects of the event.
